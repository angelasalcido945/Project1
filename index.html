About Me

My name is Angela and here are some of my hobbies:

  • Reading
  • Drawing
  • Video Games

Reading

I used to hate reading back in elementary!! My reading level was below average and I was struggling to read my assigned books. I was put into reading tutoring to help and it really did help. The tutoring definitely allowed me to appreciate and enjoy books. Since then, it opened up a rabbit hole into all sorts of reading material. I really got into:

  • Manga
  • Novels
  • Webtoons
  • Manhwas

All this new material soon led to developing favorite characters and falling in love with world building. An example of this would be my current favorite manhwa, Omnicient Readers Viewpoint!! My favorite character is of course the MC Kim Dokja! There's more about ORV and Dokja if you visit Kim Dokja & More.

Drawing

I've been drawing since I was a kid, specifically since elementary. I later joined an art club in middle school, slowly building my skills and meeting others with the same interests in art. Once I got into highschool, I started taking real art classes and learned even more! Now that I'm in college and pursuing CS, art has continued to be my biggest hobbie that I spend the most time on. You can visit My Art Pieces to see some pieces I've done! :) (They're not that great, some are old and some are recent LOL)

Video Games

I was exposed to video games from a young age. My older brother would save up all his money to get his PlayStation consoles and games. Around this time, I never really played any games, but I would always watch him play everything! His favorite series was Kingdom Hearts and I remember finding that game to be my favorite to watch.
As I got older, I took an interest to actually start playing my brothers games. I started out with Call of Duty Black Ops II zombies. I'm surprised the graphics of the zombies didn't scare me back then.
My brother finally got the money to buy our first PC that we shared (my older brother, younger brother, and I) and I remember mainly playing Roblox and Maple Story 2.
